## Part 1: Discovery & Data Audit

### Step 1: Stakeholder Interviews

**Step Overview:** Conduct structured interviews with key stakeholders to understand current state, pain points, and desired outcomes.

**Who to Interview:**
- Executive sponsor (CRO, VP Sales, VP Marketing)
- RevOps Manager / team
- Sales leadership
- Marketing operations
- Customer Success leadership

**Questions to Ask:**
- What does the current process look like today?
- Where are the biggest bottlenecks?
- What tools are currently in use?
- What does "good" look like in 6 months?
- What has been tried before and why did it fail?

### Step 2: Current State Assessment

**Step Overview:** Document the existing process, tools, data quality, and gaps before designing the solution.

- Map current workflow end-to-end
- Identify manual steps that can be automated
- Audit data quality in relevant CRM objects
- Document tool integrations and data flows
- Quantify time spent on manual processes

## Part 2: Solution Design

### Step 4: Define Requirements

- Functional requirements (what the solution must do)
- Non-functional requirements (performance, security, scale)
- Integration requirements (which systems connect)
- Reporting requirements (what metrics to track)

### Step 5: Select Approach

**Common Approaches:**
- **Approach A (Full Build):** Complete implementation across all segments
- **Approach B (Phased Rollout):** Start with one segment, expand
- **Approach C (Quick Win):** Minimum viable implementation first

**Decision Criteria:**
- Timeline and urgency
- Available resources
- Data readiness
- Organizational change capacity
