# Attribution Overview

Lead & Opportunity Attribution - Technical & Strategic Attribution Implementation

## 1. Project Overview

### What is Lead & Opportunity Attribution?

**Definition:** A technical and strategic project that implements a multi-touch attribution model to track and assign credit to marketing and sales activities that contribute to lead generation and opportunity creation. The project connects CRM, marketing automation, and web analytics data to provide visibility into which campaigns, channels, and touchpoints drive pipeline and revenue.

**What it is NOT:** Not a marketing automation platform implementation (that's a prerequisite). Not a lead lifecycle/stage definition project (separate project). Not a reporting dashboard build (attribution provides the data model; reporting is a downstream deliverable). Not a budget allocation exercise (attribution is backward-looking and informs but does not prescribe spend).

### What Attribution Unlocks

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Marketing can't prove ROI | Clear pipeline contribution by campaign/channel |
| Vanity metrics only (clicks, impressions) | Revenue attribution to touchpoints |
| Siloed data across systems | Unified view of buyer journey |
| Sales/marketing finger-pointing | Shared understanding of lead quality |
| Gut-feel budget decisions | Data-informed channel investment |

**What you get:**
- A unified attribution model that assigns credit to touchpoints across the buyer journey
- Clear visibility into which activities drive MQLs, opportunities, and closed-won revenue
- Data foundation for optimizing marketing spend toward highest-performing channels
- Shared language between sales and marketing about what's working

### ICP Value Proposition

**Pain it solves:** Marketing cannot prove ROI on campaigns because touchpoint data is siloed across CRM, marketing automation, and web analytics. Leadership asks "which campaigns are working?" and marketing can only point to vanity metrics (clicks, impressions) rather than pipeline contribution. Sales and marketing finger-point about lead quality because there's no shared view of the buyer journey.

**Outcome delivered:** A unified attribution model that assigns credit to touchpoints across the buyer journey, enabling marketing to report on pipeline contribution by campaign/channel/source. Clear visibility into which activities drive MQLs, opportunities, and closed-won revenue. Data foundation for optimizing marketing spend toward highest-performing channels.

### Who Owns It

**VP Marketing or Director of Demand Gen** (with RevOps as technical implementer)

**Key Stakeholders:**
- **VP Marketing / Director of Demand Gen** - Executive sponsor, owns outcomes
- **RevOps Manager** - Technical owner, implements CRM configuration
- **Marketing Ops** - Owns UTM standards, form tracking, campaign setup
- **Sales Leadership** - Validates that attribution reflects reality
